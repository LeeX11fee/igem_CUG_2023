Principal Investigator

Yidan Hu

Associate Professor
Dr. Hu is an Associate Professor in the School of Environmental Studies, China University of Geosciences (CUG), Wuhan, China. She obtained her Ph.D. degree in Environment Science and Engineering from Singapore Centre for Environmental Life Sciences Engineering (SCELSE), Nanyang Technological University, Singapore. Since she joined CUG in 2020, she her research efforts had been focused on exploring the application of synthetic biology in the Geoscience studies, especially in microbe-mineral interactions.

Liang Shi

Associate Professor
Dr. Shi is a Distinguished Professor in School of Environmental Studies, CUG. He obtained Ph.D. degree from Wright State University, U.S. He is one of top scientists who focus on the study of microbial extracellular electron transfer. Currently, his research efforts are focused on the application of synthetic biology approaches in electroactive biofilm engineering.

Yiran Dong

Associate Professor
Dr. Dong is a Full Professor in School of Environmental Studies, CUG. She obtained her Ph.D. degree from University of Oklahoma, U.S. Her research interests include geomicrobiology, biogeochemistry, cultivation and physiology of unclassical metal-metabolizing organisms and bioremediation of groundwater and soil contamination.

Yongguang Jiang

Professor
Dr. Jiang, an Associate Professor from School of Environmental Studies, CUG. He got his Ph.D. from Institute of Hydrobiology of Chinese Academy of Sciences, China. Dr. Jiang mainly focuses on the study of environmental microbiology, molecular microbial ecology, modification of microbial synthetic biology and its environmental application
