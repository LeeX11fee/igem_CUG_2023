Team

iGEM NCKU Tainan is established in the year of 2015. This year, with a common goal to help in tackling a real-world problem, the new team members gathered in January. We all come from different departments and work as a multidisciplinary team. Despite the imperfections in each and every one of us, we are a team full of people with amazing qualities. We are prepared to embrace our iGEM journey together, until the end.

Principal Investigator

Dr. I-Son Ng

Associate Professor - Department of Chemical Engineering
Dr. Ng is devoted to the study of synthetic biology, genetic and enzymatic engineering, biorefinery and bioremediation. She has developed a robust and efficient platform to explore the novel and functional enzymes by genetic and proteomics approach.

Dr. I-Hsiu Huang

Associate Professor - Department of Microbiology and Immunology
Dr. Huang aims to identify novel therapeutic and preventive measures against Clostridium difficile, a leading cause of antibiotic-associated diarrhea worldwide. He is also working on identifying the role of oral microbiome in oral cancer progression and proliferation.

Dr. Masayuki Hashimoto

Associate Professor - Institute of Molecular Medicine
I'm a bacterial genetist, majorly study E. coli and B. subtilis. It's my pleasure to join the NCKU iGEM team. I'm really enjoying the project with young students. They are so active, so I'm also stimulated by them. In addition, I sometimes enjoy triathlon race.

Dr. Han-Ching Wang

Professor - Department of Biotechnology and Bioindustry Sciences
Dr. Wang has a goal of using a science-based approach to manage and optimize aquaculture practices. She believes that this is very important for the eco-friendly sustainable shrimp aquaculture industry.
